Introduction of Rules

We have just some basic rules:

  • Play PI Live.
  • Have a Line account: you will need to have Line and join our Line group so it is easier to agree a match date.
  • Playing with the AI is not allowed.
  • Before the league starts you indicate your starting rotation.
  • Rotation cannot be changed during the league but you can replace a starting pitcher with a new one (just in case you buy a new pitcher on the market).
  • Bullpen and batter lineup may vary from game to game and need not to be fixed.
  • Starting Pitchers can only be in SP slots, Relief Pitchers can only be in RP slots and Closing Pitchers can only be in CP slots.
  • League games are 4 innings only.
  • Games do not necessarily need to be played in order, but the match ups need to respect the set rotation.
  • When playing 4 innings you cannot substitute your starting pitcher until the 7th inning.
  • A tie is a tie.
  • There is a mercy rule: when a team scores 15 runs, the game is over.
  • There are no rematches and a matchup is only played once.
  • When leagues finish we continue with the play-offs.
  • If teams finish with the same % the ranking will be decided by (in order):
    • Total wins.
    • Total ties.
    • Total runs.
  • To become eligible for play-offs a team will need to have played at least 70% of its matchups.
  • The first four teams of each league will continue to the play-offs.
  • The other teams of each league will continue to a final round as well.
  • In the play-offs the team ranked first of one league will play the team ranked fifth of the other league and so on.
  • In the play-offs teams need to agree between them how many games will they play to decide the play-off and how many innings each game will have.
  • In the play-offs starting rotation needs to be respected again, i.e. in game one players use their first starting pitcher, in game two their second and so on.
  • The team that ended best in its league will always have home advantage over the opponent.
