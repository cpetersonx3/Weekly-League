Introduction of Rules

We have just some basic rules:

  • Play PI Live.
  • Have a KIK account: you will need to have KIK and join our KIK group so it is easier to agree a match date.
  • Playing with the AI is not allowed.
  • It is not allowed to play with the Splitter (SPL) nor knucklecurve (K-CRV) since these pitches are considered broken UNLESS the two teams that play each other agree to use it in their match.
  • Before the league starts you indicate your starting rotation.
  • Rotation cannot be changed during the league but you can replace a starting pitcher with a new one (just in case you buy a new pitcher on the market).
  • Bullpen and batter lineup may vary from game to game and need not to be fixed.
  • Starting Pitchers can only be in SP slots, Relief Pitchers can only be in RP slots and Closing Pitchers can only be in CP slots.
  • League games are 4 innings only.
  • Games do not necessarily need to be played in order, but the match ups need to respect the set rotation.
  • When playing 4 innings you cannot substitute your starting pitcher until the 7th inning.
  • A tie is a tie.
  • There is a mercy rule: when a team scores 15 runs, the game is over.
  • When leagues finish we continue with the next league round.
  • The first five teams of each league will continue to the next round.
  • The other teams of each league will continue to a final round as well.
  • The number one and two of the final round will play a single play-off of 9 innings to decide the winner.
  • If teams finish with the same % the ranking will be decided by (in order):
    • Total wins.
    • Total ties.
    • Total runs.
  • In the final game teams are free to decide which starting pitcher to use.
