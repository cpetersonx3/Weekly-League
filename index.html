Introduction of Rules

We have just some basic rules:

  • Play PI Live.
  • Have a KIK account: you will need to have KIK and join our KIK group so it is easier to agree a match date.
  • A league will consist out of 5 teams.
  • Before the league starts you indicate your starting rotation.
  • Rotation cannot be changed during the league but you can replace a starting pitcher with a new one (just in case you buy a new pitcher on the market).
  • You can change the rotation again before the playoffs start, but afterwards the rotation is fixed again.
  • Bullpen and batter lineup may vary from game to game and need not to be fixed.
  • In each league we only play each other once.
  • Games are 4 innings only.
  • When leagues finish we continue with the playoffs.
  • Once in the playoffs we do a best of 3 unless the response to this event is so overwhelming we need to implement the wild card system, that game will be one game only.
  • Leagues will be organized the following way:
    • Teams will be distributed along the leagues depending on their OVR so the best teams are not all grouped together.
    • If possible we will try to distribute teams according to their MLB league (AL/NL) and division (West/Central/East).
    • Teams will be seeded according to their OVR and the lowest seeded teams will play 3 home matches, the highest teams will play 2 home matches.
    • We might take into account time zone as well.
